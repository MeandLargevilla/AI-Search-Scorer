Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

TL;DR: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Your search queries are sent directly to AI providers (OpenAI/Gemini) for real-time scoring only. Your API keys are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

AI Search Filter is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII) - No names, email addresses, IP addresses, or user identifiers.
  • Browsing History - We do not track or log your browsing activity.
  • Search Queries - Your search keywords are NOT stored by us or sent to any server we control.
  • API Keys - Your API keys (OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek) are stored exclusively in your local browser storage (chrome.storage.local) and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. How Your Data is Used

When you use AI Search Filter, the following data processing occurs entirely on your device or with third-party AI providers:

  • Search Keywords: Your search query text is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you configured (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or DeepSeek) for real-time relevance scoring. This transmission is temporary and used solely to generate search result scores.
  • Search Result Snippets: Text snippets from search results are sent to AI providers alongside your query to calculate relevance scores. No full page content is transmitted.
  • Caching: Scores for previously analyzed URLs are cached locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) for 7 days to reduce API calls and improve performance. This cache remains on your device only.

3. Third-Party Services

AI Search Filter integrates with the following third-party AI providers. When you use this extension, your search data is sent to the provider(s) you have configured:

  • Google Gemini API (generativelanguage.googleapis.com)
  • OpenAI API (api.openai.com)
  • DeepSeek API (api.deepseek.com)

Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data sent to their APIs:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

All extension settings and cached data are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.local). This includes:

  • Your selected AI provider (Gemini, OpenAI, or DeepSeek)
  • Your API keys (encrypted by Chrome's storage mechanism)
  • Cached search result scores (URL + score + reason, stored for 7 days)

Important: This data remains on your device and is never synchronized to any external server operated by AI Search Filter developers.

5. Permissions Explained

AI Search Filter requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage - To save your API keys, settings, and cached scores locally.
  • host_permissions (google.com) - To inject the scoring UI into Google Search result pages.
  • host_permissions (AI APIs) - To send scoring requests to Gemini, OpenAI, or DeepSeek.

We do not request permissions for tabs, history, or any other browsing data.

6. Data Retention

  • Cached Scores: Automatically deleted after 7 days or when you clear browser data.
  • API Keys: Stored until you manually remove them or uninstall the extension.
  • Extension Settings: Stored until you uninstall the extension.

7. Security

We take security seriously:

  • All API requests are sent over HTTPS.
  • API keys are stored using Chrome's secure storage mechanism.
  • No data is ever sent to servers controlled by AI Search Filter developers.

8. Children's Privacy

AI Search Filter does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
